* Context

WP-0014 (story browser) is too broad for a single executor — it spans
data curation, ClojureScript app development, ecological survey research,
and emergent dimension annotation. This DP decomposes WP-0014 into four
WPs and sequences them as a DAG.

WP-0014 remains as the vision document. It is not executed directly —
the DP's WPs are the executable units. WP-0014's acceptance criteria
are met when all four WPs in this chain complete.

* WPs in this chain

| WP | Title | Executor | Type |
|------+-------+----------+------|
| 0015 | Story data in EDN | mu2tau + mayadev | simple |
| 0016 | Ecological web survey | epistem-guardian | simple |
| 0020 | Browser shell (Phase 1) | sutradhar-guardian | simple |
| 0014 | Emergent dimension annotations | mu2tau (human) | simple |

WP-0014 is reused for its final phase — the human annotation of
emergent dimensions. Its specification section already describes
the seven dimensions. When the browser shell exists and the ecological
survey feeds it, the human annotates each story along each dimension.

* Notes

- WP-0014 (the vision document) remains at =workpacks/0014-story-browser.org=.
  Its specification and emergent dimensions sections are the reference
  for all four WPs.
- WP-0015 is small — 12 stories, hand-curated EDN. Could be done in
  a single session.
- WP-0016 parallels WP-0012/0013 (Diamer surveys) but focuses on
  ecological chains rather than regional geography. May reuse survey
  material from those WPs.
- WP-0020 is the build. It forks from the constellation viewer
  (=modules/sutradhar/website/project-constellation/=) and adapts the
  rendering for story data.
- WP-0014's final phase depends on seeing the browser — the emergent
  dimensions cannot be annotated in the abstract. The human needs to
  see stories projected into the space before judging what emerged.
