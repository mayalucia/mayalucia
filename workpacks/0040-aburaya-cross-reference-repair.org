* Context and Motivation

The spirit registry (aburaya/) has grown organically through commissioning
acts. Cross-references between spirit identity files and guild YAML files
have drifted. This creates identity confusion: a spirit may claim a guild
that does not list it, or a guild may reference a spirit that has no
identity file.

The audit was triggered by WP-0038 (dixa guild sweep) and WP-0039
(aburaya cross-reference audit). The gaps block dixa from performing
its verification function and confuse newly commissioned spirits about
their organisational home.

* What This Proves

Organisational artifacts degrade through accretion. Spirits are
commissioned, guilds are updated, cross-references drift. A project
that runs autonomous agents needs machinery to detect and repair this
drift — not as a one-time fix, but as a recurring hygiene practice.

This WP is the first instantiation of that practice. The pattern it
establishes (audit → repair → announce) can be formalised into a dixa
power (verify-scan) that runs before each commissioning act.
