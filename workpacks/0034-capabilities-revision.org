* Specification

** Governing principle

Write in plain English throughout. No industry protocol names (MCP,
A2A), no harness-specific paths (=~/.claude/skills/=,
=~/.doom.d/gptel-agents/=), no tool-specific terminology. Describe
/what the pattern is/, not /what external framework names it/. WP-0045
tests whether substrates can supply industry terminology themselves.

#+begin_insight
Plain English is not a stylistic choice — it is the portability
mechanism. A document written in one vendor's terminology is
implicitly coupled to that vendor's training data. A document written
in plain English is coupled to nothing except the reader's
comprehension. The same logic that separates powers from skills
(harness-independent from harness-specific) applies to the document
that describes them.
#+end_insight
