* Thesis

Spirit identity and organisational powers are substrate-independent.

* What This Epic Is

An epic bundles work packages that share a thesis. It is not a WP
(too broad), not a dispatch plan (those sequence WPs into DAGs), and
not a project plan (those have timelines). An epic names the question
that a cluster of WPs, together, answer.

This epic contains the work that establishes, validates, and applies
the substrate portability claim across MāyāLucIA.

* The Argument, in Stages

** Stage 1: Foundation — decouple identity from substrate

Before you can test portability, you must /define/ it. What is a
spirit? What is a harness? What is a power?

Can one spirit's understanding bootstrap another? Do orientation notes
survive cross-lineage transfer?
