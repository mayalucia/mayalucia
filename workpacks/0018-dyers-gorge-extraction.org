* Specification

Produce the following outputs:

** 1. Concept table

Org table with columns: System Concept | Story Equivalent | Notes

Identify what system-level concepts story #3 translates into the
valley's language. Not what it mentions — what it /translates/.
The test: does the story give the concept a new name, a new body,
a new behaviour in the valley's register?

Pay attention to:
- The relationship between pigment and measurement
- Whether the embedded Python code translates a system concept
  (e.g., data pipeline, validation, domain schema) or is purely
  illustrative
- The altitude-gradient structure — does it translate something
  about the project's architecture?
- Any concepts shared with other stories (especially story #4's
  instruments and story #10's mechanisms)

** 2. Character list

Who appears? What role do they play? Does the Thread Walker appear,
and if so, in what capacity?

** 3. Setting details

Confirm or correct the scaffold entry:
- Region: Parvati Valley
- Places: Parvati gorge, Manikaran, Pin Parvati
- Altitude range
- Season

** 4. Notes

Anything unusual that the browser should surface — especially the
code-as-story pattern (Python embedded in narrative).
