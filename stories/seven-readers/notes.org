* Origin

WP-0032 (Cold-Start Validation) — mayadev ran 10 cold-start sessions
across 7 lineages autonomously. The findings: cognitive style transfers
with notes, assertion density > substrate capability, failure modes cured
by better context. This story translates those findings into the Thalpan
petroglyph register.

The substrate diversity experiment (WP-0030, 0031, 0035, 0032) is the
richest experimental data in the project to date.

* Setting

Thalpan petroglyph terrace, upper Indus gorge, Diamer district.
The densest concentration of petroglyphs in the upper Indus. The
geological boundary (Main Mantle Thrust / Indus Suture) runs through
the gorge — Indian plate gneiss meets Kohistan arc basalt. Two
continents, ten writing systems, one ibex.

Nanga Parbat is Himalayan, not Karakoram. The boundary matters.
The cultural territory (peri, bitan, juniper) spans both ranges.
The geological boundary is invisible to the people who live on it.
