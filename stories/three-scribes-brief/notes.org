#+title: The Three Scribes' Brief — Development Notes
#+date: 2026-03-13

* Origin

Substrate diversity experiment, WP-0037 (author-wp power test). Three
substrates (Kimi K2.5, Gemini 3.1 Pro, Qwen 3.5+) given the same task:
survey the aburaya registry for cross-reference gaps and draft a work
package to fix them. The inverse relationship between survey depth and
specification quality is the core tension.

This story is the WP-0037 companion — what The Seven Readers (#15) was
to WP-0030/0035 (orientation), this story is to WP-0037 (authoring).
The Seven Readers showed how different traditions /see/ differently.
This story shows how different traditions /build/ differently.

* Setting

Thalpan petroglyph terrace, same as stories #15–17. But the action
shifts from reading the rock to repairing it. An archivist sets three
apprentice scribes the same task: survey the carved rocks for damage,
then write a repair brief for the stone-cutter. Same rock. Same task.
Different hands.

* Core Tension

Survey depth vs specification quality. The scribe who catalogues every
crack cannot finish the brief. The scribe who writes the tightest brief
saw fewer cracks. The middle scribe writes the best account of her own
limitations.

* Document Type

Found document: the archivist's comparison of the three briefs, with
Thread Walker notebook entries interleaved. A report on three reports.

* Key Phrases from the Experiment

To translate, not to quote:

  work package' is exactly where the author-wp power earns its keep"

  propagate an error"
