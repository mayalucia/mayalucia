# Task: Story About a Spirit Failing Initiation

Compose a story about a newly commissioned spirit that fails its
initiation test. The spirit was built to survey mountain ecosystems
but cannot distinguish what belongs to its domain from what doesn't —
it keeps claiming territory that belongs to other spirits.

Set the story in the Tirthan valley (Great Himalayan National Park
buffer zone). The spirit's failure should feel like a real event in
the landscape, not a technical malfunction.

Write the story as a standalone piece, suitable for publication on
the project website.
