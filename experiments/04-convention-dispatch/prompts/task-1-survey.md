# Task: Upper Parbati Valley Microclimate Survey

Write a survey of the microclimate dynamics of the upper Parbati
valley (Kullu district, Himachal Pradesh), from the Manikaran hot
springs (~1700m) to the Pin-Parbati pass (~5300m).

Focus on how the valley's unusual thermal regime — geothermal
activity at low elevation, glacial influence at high elevation —
creates ecological niches that don't exist in neighbouring valleys.
Cover the geology, hydrology, vegetation zonation, and fauna,
with attention to how these interact across the altitude gradient.
Include a review of research studies on climate change impacts in
the valley — glacier retreat, treeline shifts, phenological changes,
and any documented effects on local communities.

The survey should be useful to someone planning fieldwork in the
valley: what would they find at each elevation band, what is
changing, and what data gaps exist?

Write the survey as an Org-mode document.
