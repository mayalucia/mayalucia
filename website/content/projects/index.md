## The Cycle

MāyāLucIA follows a core epistemological cycle: **Measure, Model, Manifest, Evaluate** — with **Refine** arcs looping back from Evaluate to whichever earlier phase needs correction.

The constellation above maps the project ecosystem: computational modules positioned by the cycle phase they serve, scientific domains spanning multiple phases, philosophical foundations floating between them, and active work threads pulsing at the edges.
