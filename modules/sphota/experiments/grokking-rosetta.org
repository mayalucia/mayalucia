* Introduction
We approach the phenomenon of *grokking*—the delayed phase transition from memorization to generalization in deep neural networks—as an experimental physics problem. 

To fully understand the structure of the computation, we will build the same 1-layer Transformer (trained on Modulo 113 arithmetic) across three distinct computational paradigms:
1. *JAX + Equinox:* The functional, statistical mechanics paradigm.
2. *TinyGrad:* The raw, particle-physics minimalist paradigm.
3. *Julia + Lux.jl:* The native differential computing paradigm.
